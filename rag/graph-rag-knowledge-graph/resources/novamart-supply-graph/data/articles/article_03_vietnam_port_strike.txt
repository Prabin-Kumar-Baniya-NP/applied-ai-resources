Port Workers' Strike Enters Second Week in Hanoi

HANOI — A labor strike by dockworkers has stalled cargo handling at key ports serving the
Hanoi manufacturing corridor for the second consecutive week. Assembly and casing suppliers
operating in the Hanoi region report growing backlogs of both inbound materials and outbound
finished components.

Negotiations between the workers' union and port operators are ongoing. The disruption is
rated moderate to high, with exporters warning of knock-on delays to downstream customers.
