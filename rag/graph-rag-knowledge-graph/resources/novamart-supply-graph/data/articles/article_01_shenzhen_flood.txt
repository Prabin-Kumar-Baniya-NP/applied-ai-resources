Severe Flooding Paralyzes Shenzhen Manufacturing District

SHENZHEN — Record monsoon rainfall over the past 72 hours has caused severe flooding
across Shenzhen's industrial zones, submerging factory floors and halting production at
several electronics component plants. Local authorities report that logistics and port
operations in the region are expected to remain disrupted for at least two weeks.

Manufacturers in the area have warned customers of significant shipment delays. Analysts
say component suppliers based in Shenzhen are among the hardest hit, with battery cell and
wireless module production lines offline. The severity of the disruption is considered high.
